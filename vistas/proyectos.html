COCA-COLA LONG RUN 2022

La carrera de larga distancia más extensa en islas maldivas Conoce más

Únete al recorrido!

Coca-cola Longrun is one of the biggest distant running event in the Maldives. the classic 5.3km route will blow your mind with its planned activities to ensure you keep running.
Longer. Faster. and yes, slower as well. In 2018, the expo was an all day long music festival in the event village itself. In 2018, we introduced DJ corners making three total DJ stations around the loop. In 2018, we introduced moving volunteers around the loop to ensure you are well taken care of. And in 2018, we started the event one hour early (2:20am for marathoners) to ensure you finish well before the sun makes you exhausted!
